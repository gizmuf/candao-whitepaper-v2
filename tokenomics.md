## Buyback & Make Strategy

**No token burning.** All revenue:
1. Converts to $CDO on open market
2. Redistributes to ecosystem participants
3. Creates continuous buying pressure

**Guardrails:**

| Parameter | Constraint |
|-----------|------------|
| Max buyback per cycle | 5% of treasury |
| Frequency | Monthly |
| Distribution | Pro-rata to stakers/LPs |
| Emergency pause | DAO vote (48h timelock) |
| Audit | Quarterly third-party verification |
