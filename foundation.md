# Candao Foundation

**Candao Foundation (CF)** is a Dubai-based organization launching the Zero Fee Economy.

## Core Functions

### 1. Decentralized VC
- DAO-governed investment
- Community evaluates projects
- Transparent allocation

### 2. Accelerator
- Mentorship
- Technical resources
- Go-to-market support

### 3. App Factory
- Build production apps for partners
- **$1,000 CDO hold = full app delivery**
- Revenue split: 70% partner / 30% CF

## Partner Revenue Model

| Revenue Component | Partner Share | CF Share |
|-------------------|--------------|----------|
| One-time user payments | 70% | 30% |
| Subscription revenue | 70% | 30% |
| Transaction fees | N/A (zero fee) | N/A |

## 5 Challenges, One Mission

Candao Foundation mobilizes global talent through:

1. **Partners Challenge** — Build zero-fee platform alternatives
2. **Builders Challenge** — Smart contract & Ghost hackathons
3. **Creators Challenge** — Content & marketing competitions
4. **Ambassadors Challenge** — Global community expansion
5. **Innovators Challenge** — AI & infrastructure R&D
