## What Candao Is

- **AI-first** — Autonomous agents (Ghosts), not chatbots
- **Agentic** — Ghosts act, negotiate, and close deals on your behalf
- **Post-app** — Infinite Interface replaces siloed applications
- **Zero-fee** — One-time token holding, no recurring extraction

## What Candao Is NOT

- ❌ **Not a traditional marketplace** — No listings, no browsing, Ghosts find matches
- ❌ **Not a SaaS platform** — No subscriptions, no monthly fees
- ❌ **Not a speculative meme token** — Utility-first, tied to real economic activity
- ❌ **Not an AI black box** — User-controlled permissions, explainable actions, on-chain audit trails

## Executive Summary

Candao is building the **Zero Fee Economy** — a paradigm shift where platform fees, subscription costs, and intermediary commissions become obsolete.

| Traditional Platforms | Candao |
